About Guardian

How does one stay safe? Parents teach their children “Stranger Danger” to keep them safe. College campuses populate their sidewalks with Blue Light Phones for increased security for students. Women traveling alone are told to carry pepper spray concealed as lipstick in their purse. But in spite of precautions and alertness, terrible incidents are still apt to happen. How can we increase protection and security when bad things happen? How can we give users tools for action in situations that might be out of their control? Can we utilize technology that individuals have on hand to keep them safe, through location pins, automatic calls to police, texts to emergency contacts, or other methods? Our goal is to conduct interviews and collect feedback from late night runners to city travelers in order to design an effective safety technology.
